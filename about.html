Your Digital Hostel Companion

Welcome to the NBKRIST Hostel Portal, designed to make your stay simple, smart, and seamless.

Your Journey Through the Portal

Step 1: Settle In

Log in and visit your **Profile** page. Fill in your details so the administration knows who you are. This is your digital ID card for the hostel.

Step 2: Stay Informed

Your **Dashboard** is your best friend. Check it daily for the latest news on the **Notice Board** and to see what's on the **Mess Menu** for the day.

Step 3: Take Action

Need a room? Go to **Room Booking**. Have a problem? File a **Complaint**. All your needs are just a click away.
